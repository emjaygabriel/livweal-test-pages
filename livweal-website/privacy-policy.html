Privacy Policy

At LivWeal, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect certain information about you, such as your name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide. We may also automatically collect information about your device and usage of our website, such as your IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit.

Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve our website and services, and to communicate with you. For example, we may use your information to respond to your inquiries, send you promotional materials, and personalize your experience on our website.

Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate our website or provide services to our customers. For example, we may use a third-party email marketing platform to send promotional emails to our subscribers. We may also disclose your information in response to legal process, to protect our rights or property, or to protect the safety of others.

Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your usage of our website and to provide you with a better experience. For example, we may use cookies to remember your preferences and to help us analyze how users navigate our website. You can adjust your browser settings to disable cookies, but this may affect your ability to use our website.

Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites that are not owned or operated by LivWeal. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Security of Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no security measure is 100% effective, and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time by posting a new version on our website. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.
